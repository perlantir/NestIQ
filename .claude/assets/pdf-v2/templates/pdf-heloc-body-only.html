Analyst narrative

AI-generated · reviewed

The {{borrower_last}}s' existing first mortgage at {{current_rate_pct}} is a rate that will not be available again in this cycle. Refinancing the entire balance to access {{cash_need_formatted}} would reset the full loan to {{cashout_refi_rate_pct}} — a {{rate_premium_bps}} bps premium on {{first_mortgage_balance_formatted}}, not just on the cash drawn.

A HELOC isolates the marginal borrowing at today's rate while preserving the first-lien economics. Blended cost of capital is {{blended_effective_rate_pct}} — materially below a full refi.

Principal risk: the HELOC rate is variable. Page 3 models the payment under +100 bps and +200 bps shocks; the borrower retains the option to refinance the HELOC into a fixed second or roll it into a cash-out refi later if rates decline.
